Adapting to the AI Landscape

How can I continue my education with AI specializations in Education?

Pursue learning engineering or educational technology certifications from programs at Carnegie Mellon, Georgia Tech, or Harvard Graduate School of Education. Specialized courses in AI for education from organizations like ISTE provide practitioner-focused training.

What conferences should I attend for Education AI?

Attend ASU+GSV Summit, ISTE Conference, the AI in Education Summit, and Learning @ Scale. These events bring together education leaders, edtech founders, and AI researchers focused on transforming learning.

How can I grow as an AI leader within Education?

Education AI leaders grow by conducting research on AI learning outcomes, contributing to education AI ethics guidelines, and building partnerships between schools, universities, and technology companies. Mentoring students in AI builds the pipeline while demonstrating leadership.
